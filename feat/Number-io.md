This feature marks number agreement of the verb with the indirect object or of postposition with its noun.

### <a name="Sing">`Sing`</a>: Singular

#### Examples

* [abq] _хI-<b>и</b>-цIгIа-пI_ [1PL.ABS-<b>3SG.M.IO</b>-ask-NPST.DCL] ‘we'll ask <b>him</b>’ `Gender[io]=Masc|Number[abs]=Plur|Number[io]=Sing|Person[abs]=1|Person[io]=3|Tense=Fut2|VerbForm=Fin`

### <a name="Plur">`Plur`</a>: Plural

#### Examples

* [abq] _д-гIа-<b>ра</b>-з-цIгIа-тI_ [3SG.H.ABS-CSL-<b>3PL.IO</b>-BEN-ask-DCL] ‘he asked <b>them</b>’ `Gender[abs]=Com|Number[abs]=Sing|Number[io]=Plur|Person[abs]=3|Person[io]=3|Tense=Aor|VerbForm=Fin`
